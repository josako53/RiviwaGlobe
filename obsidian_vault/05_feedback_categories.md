# Feedback Categories

Categories help classify feedback by the type of issue. The AI uses these to route feedback to the right team.

## Infrastructure / Construction Projects

**compensation**
Complaints or questions about financial compensation for land, crops, property, or livelihoods affected by the project.
Examples: "I have not received my compensation payment", "The amount offered for my land is too low"

**resettlement**
Issues related to physical displacement and relocation of households or businesses.
Examples: "The resettlement house is smaller than promised", "We have been moved to a location without water"

**land-acquisition**
Problems specifically with the process of acquiring land for the project.
Examples: "My land was taken without proper valuation", "I was not informed about the acquisition"

**construction-impact**
Impacts caused by active construction activities.
Examples: "Construction dust is unbearable", "Noise from night works is disturbing our sleep", "Blasting cracked my house walls"

**traffic**
Traffic disruption, road closures, detours, and access issues caused by construction.
Examples: "The road to my shop is blocked for 2 weeks", "The detour route is dangerous"

**worker-rights**
Issues about workers on the project — safety, conduct, fair treatment.
Examples: "Workers are not wearing safety gear", "A contractor worker harassed a woman in our community"

**safety-hazard**
Unsafe conditions created by the project.
Examples: "There is an open trench near the school with no barriers", "Construction materials are blocking emergency access"

**environmental**
Environmental damage, pollution, or ecological harm.
Examples: "The river is now muddy from the construction", "Trees were cut beyond the project boundary", "Chemical spill near the borehole"

## Project Management

**engagement**
Issues with community consultation, information sharing, and stakeholder participation.
Examples: "We were not consulted before construction started", "The community meeting was not held"

**design-issue**
Technical or design problems with the project.
Examples: "The road drainage was designed wrong and water now floods our area"

**project-delay**
Delays in project implementation.
Examples: "Construction was supposed to start 6 months ago and nothing has happened"

**corruption**
Allegations of bribery, fraud, or misuse of project resources.
Examples: "The contractor is paying off local officials", "Materials were stolen from the site"

**communication**
Poor communication or lack of information from the project team.
Examples: "Nobody tells us what is happening or when construction will finish"

## Services & Operations

**accessibility**
Inability to access services, facilities, or locations.
Examples: "The health centre is now inaccessible after the road was closed"

**quality**
Quality of delivered goods or services.
Examples: "The road surface is already cracking after 3 months"

**timeliness**
Speed and timeliness of service delivery.
Examples: "We have been waiting 3 months for the water connection"

**staff-conduct**
Behaviour or professionalism of staff or contractor personnel.
Examples: "The project officer is rude and dismissive", "Contractor workers are fighting in the community"

**community-impact**
Broader impacts on the community's social fabric and daily life.
Examples: "The project has caused conflict between our community and the neighbouring village"

**responsiveness**
Lack of response or follow-up from project/organisation staff.
Examples: "I reported this 3 times and nobody has responded"

**community-benefit**
Questions or concerns about promised community benefits.
Examples: "We were promised jobs from this project but no locals were hired"

**employment**
Labour-related issues — hiring, payment, conditions.
Examples: "Local youth applied for jobs but all workers are from outside", "Day labourers have not been paid"

**design**
General design or planning concerns.

**process**
Concerns about administrative or procedural matters.

**safety**
General safety concerns not covered by safety-hazard.

**other**
Issues that do not fit any other category.
