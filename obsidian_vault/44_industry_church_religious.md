## Suggestion / Advice Signals — Church / Religious Organization

### Financial Management
- The church should provide quarterly financial reports to all members
- Kanisa linapaswa kutoa ripoti za fedha za kila robo mwaka kwa wanachama wote
- An independent audit committee should review church accounts annually
- Kamati ya ukaguzi huru inapaswa kukagua hesabu za kanisa kila mwaka
- Establish a transparent budgeting process with member input
- Weka mchakato wa uwazi wa bajeti unaohusisha maoni ya wanachama
- Create a welfare fund specifically for members in need
- Unda mfuko wa ustawi mahsusi kwa wanachama wanaohitaji
- Tithe receipts should be issued to every member who gives
- Risiti za zaka zinapaswa kutolewa kwa kila mwanachama anayetoa

### Pastoral & Spiritual Development
- The pastor should receive formal theological training
- Mchungaji anapaswa kupata mafunzo rasmi ya theolojia
- Home visits to sick and elderly members should be scheduled regularly
- Ziara za nyumbani kwa wagonjwa na wazee zinapaswa kupangwa mara kwa mara
- Introduce counseling services for members facing personal challenges
- Anzisha huduma za ushauri nasaha kwa wanachama wanaokabiliwa na changamoto za kibinafsi
- Reduce sermon length and improve content quality
- Punguza urefu wa mahubiri na uboreshe ubora wa maudhui
- Pastors should have an open-door policy for member concerns
- Wachungaji wanapaswa kuwa na sera ya mlango wazi kwa wasiwasi wa wanachama

### Worship & Facilities
- Invest in a quality sound system for better worship experience
- Wekeza katika mfumo bora wa sauti kwa uzoefu bora wa ibada
- Install fans or air conditioning in the church hall
- Weka mashabiki au hali ya hewa katika ukumbi wa kanisa
- Build a ramp and accessible toilets for disabled members
- Jenga ramp na vyoo vinavyopatikana kwa wanachama wenye ulemavu
- Introduce online/livestream services for members who cannot attend physically
- Anzisha huduma za mtandaoni/livestream kwa wanachama wasioweza kuhudhuria kimwili
- Provide clean drinking water in the church compound
- Toa maji safi ya kunywa ndani ya eneo la kanisa

### Youth & Children Ministry
- Create a dedicated youth program with qualified mentors
- Unda mpango mahsusi wa vijana na washauri wenye sifa
- Sunday school teachers should be trained and certified
- Walimu wa shule ya Jumapili wanapaswa kufunzwa na kupata cheti
- Establish a scholarship fund to support members' children in school
- Weka mfuko wa udhamini kusaidia watoto wa wanachama shuleni
- Organize youth retreats and leadership development camps
- Panga mikutano ya vijana na kambi za maendeleo ya uongozi
- Include young people in church decision-making structures
- Jumuisha vijana katika miundo ya kufanya maamuzi ya kanisa

### Governance & Accountability
- The church constitution should be reviewed and shared with all members
- Katiba ya kanisa inapaswa kukaguliwa na kushirikiwa na wanachama wote
- Elections for church positions should be held regularly and transparently
- Uchaguzi wa nafasi za kanisa unapaswa kufanywa mara kwa mara na kwa uwazi
- A grievance committee should be established for member complaints
- Kamati ya malalamiko inapaswa kuanzishwa kwa malalamiko ya wanachama
- Leadership should declare personal interests to avoid conflicts of interest
- Uongozi unapaswa kutangaza maslahi ya kibinafsi ili kuepuka migongano ya maslahi

## Industry-Specific Escalation Triggers
- **Sexual abuse or misconduct** by clergy / pastoral staff → immediate escalation: Unyanyasaji wa kijinsia au matendo mabaya ya kimaadili na mchungaji
- **Child abuse** within Sunday school, church camp, or youth ministry → safeguarding emergency
- **Financial fraud or embezzlement** of tithes/building fund → legal/regulatory escalation
- **Cult-like coercion** — members threatened, manipulated, or prevented from leaving → urgent
- **Physical violence** during church services or between members/leadership → immediate
- **Medical emergency** during church event (crusade, camp, service) — no first aid available
- **Doctrinal manipulation** leading to members abandoning medication, education, or family
- **Forced exorcism** or harmful spiritual practices causing physical or psychological harm
- **Public shaming** of a member regarding finances, sin, or family matters from the pulpit
- **Underage marriage** facilitated or encouraged by church leadership
- **Building collapse risk** — unsafe church structure with known structural defects
- **Missing persons** — member disappears after joining a church group or cell

## Disambiguation Notes
- **Church vs. NGO**: Churches with community programs (schools, hospitals, orphanages) may generate feedback that overlaps with NGO or Education KBs — use entity signals (pastor, mchungaji, askofu, ibada) to confirm church context
- **Church vs. Event**: Wedding and burial complaints at a church are church-industry feedback, not event-industry — distinguish by presence of clergy/doctrine terms
- **Church vs. Education**: Sunday school complaints are church-industry; if feedback is about a church-run formal school, route to Education KB
- **Church vs. Personal Development**: Spiritual coaching and mentorship within a church are church-industry; secular life coaching is Personal Development
- **Mosque / Temple / Synagogue**: The same KB applies — signals like imam, msikiti, hekalu, masjid, swala, Ijumaa, Ramadhan map to this KB with religious-organization context
