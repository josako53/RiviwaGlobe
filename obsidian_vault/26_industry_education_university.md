## Suggestion / Advice Signals — Education

### Teaching & Curriculum Improvements
- Teachers should receive refresher training every year to stay updated on the syllabus
- The curriculum should include entrepreneurship education from Form 1
- Practical science lessons should be compulsory and equipped schools should be a priority
- Introduce Swahili as a medium of instruction in primary school for better comprehension
- Teachers should be deployed based on subject need, not just regional preference
- Online supplementary materials should be made available to Form IV students
- You should create a peer tutoring program to help weaker students
- Consider introducing ICT as a compulsory subject from Standard 3 in primary school

### Infrastructure & Resources
- Build more dormitories at the school so rural students can study without daily commuting
- Provide each school with a minimum functioning science lab by 2027
- Government should ensure textbook distribution reaches schools by the first week of term
- Construct proper toilet blocks especially for girl students to reduce absenteeism
- Schools in flood-prone areas should be rebuilt on higher ground
- Supply reliable solar electricity to off-grid schools for evening studies
- Equip school libraries with at least one computer and internet access per 50 students

### Financial & Fees Suggestions
- HESLB loan disbursement should be digitized and reach universities faster
- Create a bursary fund specifically for talented students from poor rural areas
- Fee structures should be frozen and only adjusted with public consultation
- School management committees should publish audited accounts annually
- Introduce a needs-based tuition waiver program at public universities
- Provide free sanitary pads to all girls in public secondary schools

### Examinations & Assessment Suggestions
- NECTA should allow online result verification through a secure student portal
- Continuous assessment should count for a higher proportion of the final grade
- Replace some written examinations with project-based assessments
- Allow students to see marked examination scripts to understand their mistakes
- Grade 7 (Standard 7) PSLE examination should be abolished to reduce pressure on children
- NECTA should publish examination results within 6 weeks of the examination date
- Provide practice past papers through an official government website for free

### University Governance Suggestions
- Research supervisors should have a maximum of 8 postgraduate students at a time
- Introduce anonymous lecturer evaluation forms at the end of every semester
- TCU should create a student transfer process that takes less than 30 days
- Create an independent student ombudsman at every public university
- University senate meetings should include elected student representative observers

## Industry-Specific Escalation Triggers

- Physical assault or corporal punishment of a student by a teacher or school official
- Sexual harassment or abuse of a student by a teacher, lecturer, or school staff member
- Examination paper leakage or confirmed cheating syndicate involving teaching staff
- Student suicide or attempted suicide linked to academic pressure, bullying, or abuse at a learning institution
- Student missing from boarding school with no notification to parents
- Confirmed embezzlement of school capitation grant or student fee funds by administration
- School or university building structural collapse or fire risk posing immediate danger to students
- Student denied emergency medical attention by a school or university
- Epidemic outbreak (cholera, typhoid, measles) in a school dormitory or campus with no response from management
- Academic staff strike lasting more than 4 weeks causing irreversible academic calendar disruption
- Confirmed NECTA examination irregularity or impersonation case at a center
- A child with a disability denied school admission or examination accommodation in violation of law

## Disambiguation Notes

- Education feedback about **HESLB loan disbursement** may resemble **Finance / Banking** complaints — classify as Education if the primary complaint is about the student loan program's administration, not about the bank's handling of funds.
- Complaints about **teachers' salaries or employment conditions** may overlap with **Labour / Employment** — classify as Education when the impact affects student learning outcomes, and as Labour when the complaint is purely about employment rights.
- **VETA and vocational training** feedback belongs in Education even though it has strong links to employment — the delivery institution (school/training center) is the classifier.
- Feedback about **student welfare and mental health** at a university overlaps with **Healthcare** — classify as Education if the institution's counseling or support services are the subject, and as Healthcare if a medical facility is the primary complainant.
- **School feeding program** complaints may superficially resemble Food / FMCG complaints — the institutional context (public school, government program) anchors them firmly in Education.
