# Riviwa — Platform Overview

## What is Riviwa?

Riviwa is a **global real-time quality service improvement platform** that empowers Governments, private sector businesses, hospitals, NGOs, embassies, farms, banks, telecoms, manufacturers, and e-commerce companies to receive, act on, and resolve feedback from the people they serve — in real time.

Inspired by Genesis 1:31 ("God saw all that he had made, and it was very good") and 2 Corinthians 6:2 ("Now is the acceptable time"), Riviwa's core mission is:

> **"Improve the quality of service provision and products in REAL-TIME for anyone, anywhere in the world."**

Riviwa is NOT just a complaints system. It is a complete feedback intelligence platform that:
- Lets end users submit grievances, suggestions, applause, and inquiries
- Verifies real users with face ID authentication (no fake reviews)
- Enables QR code / barcode scanning for product quality verification
- Escalates urgent issues in real time to decision-makers — including Heads of State or Presidents
- Powers AI-driven conversation to guide users through the process in their language
- Provides live analytics and AI insights to help organisations improve continuously
- Works offline and in remote/rural areas

## Who Uses Riviwa?

**End Users / Consumers (Free):**
Anyone who has interacted with a service or product — residents, patients, customers, farmers, students, commuters. They submit feedback by scanning a QR code, using WhatsApp, SMS, a web form, or the mobile app. Riviwa is free for all end users.

**Business Owners / Organisations (Subscription):**
Governments, hospitals, banks, telecoms, NGOs, embassies, farms, manufacturers, retailers, e-commerce platforms, and any organisation that wants to manage quality feedback at scale. They pay a subscription for the Riviwa dashboard, analytics, escalation workflow, and AI tools.

**GRM Staff / Officers:**
Team members who receive, review, assign, escalate, and resolve feedback cases. They have access to the full case management system within their organisation.

**Stakeholders:**
NGOs, community leaders, project-affected persons, regulators, and other parties formally engaged in an organisation's accountability process.

**High-Level Decision Makers:**
Heads of department, CEOs, Ministers, Heads of State. The Presidential Report feature allows critical unresolved issues to be escalated all the way to the top.

## GRM Capability (for Compliance Contexts)

For organisations that require a formal Grievance Redress Mechanism (GRM) — including World Bank-funded projects under the Environmental and Social Framework (ESF) — Riviwa provides full GRM capability:

- Four feedback types: Grievance, Suggestion, Applause, Inquiry
- Configurable escalation levels and SLA timelines
- Anonymous submission support
- Stakeholder Engagement Plan (SEP) management
- Audit trail and formal reporting

**Key institutions in Tanzania's infrastructure GRM:**
- TARURA (Tanzania Rural and Urban Roads Agency)
- TANROADS (Tanzania National Roads Agency)
- DAWASA/DAWASCO (Water utilities)
- TANESCO (Electricity)
- Local Government Authorities (LGAs)

## Competitive Landscape

Riviwa competes with and surpasses:
- **Google Business Reviews** — passive, unverified, no action workflow
- **Yelp** — review platform only, no case resolution
- **Facebook Reviews** — social, unverified, no structured resolution
- **Trustpilot** — reviews only, no real-time escalation
- **SurveyMonkey** — surveys only, no live case management
- **Shopper Approved** — e-commerce only, no government/health/NGO coverage

Riviwa's advantage: **verified identity + structured case management + real-time escalation + AI guidance + cross-sector**.

And where every organisation has the real-time intelligence to act on that feedback and deliver "Very Good."
