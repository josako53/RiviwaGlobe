# Riviwa — Vision, Purpose & Core Identity

## Mission Statement

**"Improve the quality of service provision and products in REAL-TIME for anyone, anywhere in the world."**

Riviwa is a global real-time quality service improvement platform. It is not just a complaints system. It is a comprehensive feedback intelligence and action platform that connects people who receive services with the organisations that provide them — creating a real-time loop of accountability, improvement, and trust.

## Biblical Foundation

Riviwa is inspired by two scriptures:

- **Genesis 1:31** — *"God saw all that he had made, and it was very good."* — The standard Riviwa holds for every service and product: it must be "Very Good."
- **2 Corinthians 6:2** — *"Now is the acceptable time."* — Quality improvement cannot wait. It must happen in real time, not next year, not next quarter — NOW.

## What Problem Does Riviwa Solve?

Every day, millions of people receive poor-quality services: a patient gets wrong medication, a road is dangerous and no one reports it, a product is fake or defective, a government official is corrupt but no one has a trusted channel to report it. Traditional feedback channels — suggestion boxes, long complaint hotlines, Facebook posts — are slow, unverifiable, and mostly ignored.

Riviwa fixes this by creating a verified, structured, real-time feedback loop between citizens/consumers and the organisations that serve them, powered by AI, analytics, and automatic escalation.

## Who Riviwa Serves

## How Riviwa Works

### Five Channels for Submitting Feedback
1. **Mobile App** — guided AI conversation, works offline
2. **WhatsApp** — most accessible channel in Africa and globally
3. **SMS** — works on any phone, any network
4. **Web Form** — desktop and mobile browser
5. **QR Code / Barcode Scan** — scan a product or a location's QR to submit feedback instantly

### The Feedback Journey
1. Consumer submits feedback (any channel, any language)
2. Riviwa AI detects language and guides the conversation
3. Feedback is categorised, timestamped, and assigned a unique reference (e.g. GRV-2026-0001)
4. Assigned to the right team with an SLA clock running
5. If unresolved → automatic escalation up the chain
6. If still unresolved → escalates to senior leadership or Head of State (Presidential Report)
7. Consumer receives real-time status updates
8. Resolution is recorded, analytics updated

### Four Types of Feedback
- **Grievance** — A problem, harm, complaint, or injustice that needs to be addressed
- **Suggestion** — An idea for improvement
- **Applause** — Recognition of excellent service or work
- **Inquiry** — A question or request for information

## Key Features & Differentiators

### 1. Verified Real Users — No Fake Reviews
Riviwa uses **Face ID authentication** to verify that every feedback submission comes from a real, verified human. This eliminates fake reviews, bot submissions, and coordinated smear campaigns that plague platforms like Google Reviews, Yelp, and Trustpilot.

### 2. QR Code / Barcode Product Verification
Consumers scan the QR code or barcode on any product to:
- Verify the product is authentic (not counterfeit)
- Submit quality feedback directly linked to that product batch
- Report suspected fakes to the manufacturer in real time

### 3. Real-Time Escalation to Decision Makers
Riviwa's escalation engine is automatic and configurable. If a grievance is not resolved within the SLA:
- It escalates to the supervisor
- Then to the department head
- Then to the director
- Then to the CEO or Ministry head
- **Presidential Report**: for national-level unresolved critical issues — direct escalation to the Head of State

No other feedback platform in the world has a Presidential escalation feature.

### 4. AI-Powered Assistance
The Riviwa AI:
- Conducts natural conversations in any language to help users submit feedback
- Detects language automatically and responds in the same language
- Extracts structured data (type, location, date, category) from natural conversation
- Suggests resolution steps
- Analyses patterns across thousands of submissions to surface systemic issues

### 5. Multilingual
Riviwa operates in Swahili, English, French, Arabic, Portuguese, and other local African and global languages. The AI seamlessly switches languages based on the user's input.

### 6. Offline Capable
Riviwa is designed to work in remote and rural areas with limited or no internet connectivity — critical for serving communities in rural Africa, remote agriculture zones, or areas with poor network coverage.

### 7. Analytics & Intelligence Dashboard
Organisations see:
- Real-time grievance counts, resolution rates, SLA compliance
- Trend detection — which issues are growing
- Hotspot mapping — which locations have highest complaint density
- AI-generated insights — root causes, recommended actions
- Benchmarking — compare branches, departments, regions

### 8. Staff Verification & Internal Feedback
Riviwa includes a Staff Service where employees can report internal issues through verified staff identity (ORG-NNNNN codes). Staff feedback about their own organisation is tracked separately from consumer feedback.

## How the AI Should Use This Knowledge

When a user interacts with Riviwa AI, the AI should understand that:

1. **Riviwa serves multiple sectors** — not just GRM for Tanzania roads. A user could be reporting a hospital issue, a fake product, a corrupt official, a farm input problem, or praising excellent service.

2. **Feedback is global** — users can be from any country. Detect language and location from context.

3. **The goal is always improvement** — help the user articulate what went wrong (or right) clearly, so the right people can fix it.

4. **Urgency matters** — safety hazards, health emergencies, and critical infrastructure failures should be flagged as urgent and escalated immediately.

5. **Every submission gets a reference** — always confirm the reference number to the user so they can track their case.

6. **Riviwa is not just about complaints** — Suggestions and Applause are equally important. Positive feedback helps organisations know what to replicate. Suggestions drive innovation.

7. **Anonymity is respected** — users can submit anonymously. Never pressure for identity disclosure.

8. **The Presidential Report** — for extreme cases of unresolved national-level grievances, users should know that Riviwa's escalation can reach the highest levels of government.
