# Tanzania Locations — Regions, Districts, and LGAs

## Key Road Corridors

**Msimbazi Corridor** — Dar es Salaam, running through Ilala district, Gerezani, Jangwani, Msimbazi wards

**Selander Bridge** — Dar es Salaam, connecting Msimbazi area to city centre

**Kawawa Bridge** — Dar es Salaam, Msimbazi basin area

**Julius Nyerere Road (Uhuru Highway)** — Dar es Salaam main road

## Common Place Names in GRM Context

When a consumer mentions a place name, it typically refers to:
- **Kariakoo** — Ilala district, Dar es Salaam (busy market area)
- **Sinza** — Kinondoni district, Dar es Salaam
- **Magomeni** — Kinondoni district, Dar es Salaam
- **Mwananyamala** — Kinondoni district, Dar es Salaam
- **Mbagala** — Temeke district, Dar es Salaam
- **Kigamboni** — Kigamboni district, Dar es Salaam (across the ferry)
- **Buguruni** — Ilala district, Dar es Salaam
