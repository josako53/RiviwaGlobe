## Suggestion / Advice Signals — Retail / Consumer Products

### Store Operations & Layout
- You should reorganize the shop floor so it's easier to find items
- Consider adding more clear aisle signs for different product categories
- It would help to have a self-checkout option to reduce queues on busy days
- Please consider extending operating hours on weekends
- A customer service desk near the entrance would make the experience much better
- Adding trolley escalators or elevators would improve accessibility
- Consider having a dedicated pickup section for online orders
- A loyalty program with meaningful rewards would bring more repeat customers

### Product Range & Sourcing
- You should stock more locally made products — support Tanzanian manufacturers
- It would be good to have a wider variety of healthy and organic food options
- Please consider stocking products with Swahili labeling for upcountry customers
- Introduce more affordable brands alongside the premium ones
- Stock sizes that fit African body types — current clothing selection is too limited
- You should verify TBS compliance for all imported goods before stocking
- Consider an anti-counterfeit verification process at the point of receiving stock
- A dedicated section for spare parts and accessories would be very useful

### Pricing & Payment
- Please consider accepting all mobile money platforms — not just one provider
- A buy-now-pay-later option with clear terms would help lower-income customers
- Price tags should be updated in real time to avoid discrepancies at checkout
- Introduce a senior citizen discount or a family pack pricing model
- Provide itemized receipts in both English and Swahili
- Consider offering bulk purchase discounts clearly displayed on shelves
- A price comparison tool on your website would build customer trust

### Delivery & E-commerce
- Delivery tracking via SMS would greatly improve the customer experience
- Consider partnering with local bodaboda apps for faster last-mile delivery
- Add an option for in-store pickup for online orders to avoid delivery fees
- Please allow customers to reschedule delivery via a simple WhatsApp message
- Offer package insurance for high-value items during delivery
- Build a simple USSD ordering option for customers with basic phones

### Environmental & Social Responsibility
- Please reduce plastic packaging and offer eco-friendly bag alternatives
- Consider a product take-back program for expired or damaged goods
- Support local artisans by creating a dedicated section for Tanzanian craft products

## Inquiry / Question Signals — Retail / Consumer Products

### Product Information
- Do you have this product available in a different size or colour?
- Is this brand genuine or an imitation?
- Does this item carry the Tanzania Bureau of Standards mark?
- What is the expiry date on this product?
- Is this product suitable for use in Tanzania's climate?
- Does the electronics item come with a local warranty?
- What are the washing instructions for this fabric?
- Is this furniture self-assembly or does it come already built?
- Does this product require original brand accessories or can I use alternatives?
- What are the dimensions of this item — I need to check if it fits my space?

### Pricing & Payments
- What is the price of this item in Tanzanian shillings?
- Do you accept M-Pesa or Tigo Pesa at this store?
- Is there a discount if I buy in bulk?
- Do you offer installment payments for furniture and electronics?
- Is the price on your website the same as in the physical store?
- How does your loyalty card program work?
- Are there any ongoing promotions or sales I should know about?
- What is the exchange rate you use for USD-priced goods?

### Availability & Stock
- Is this item currently available in your Dar es Salaam branch?
- When do you expect to restock this product?
- Can I reserve an item and pay when I pick it up?
- Do you transfer stock between branches if one location is out of stock?
- Is this item available for same-day delivery in Dar es Salaam?
- Can I order online and collect from the store?

### Returns & Warranty
- What is your return policy for electronics?
- If the product is faulty, do you offer a refund or store credit only?
- How do I make a warranty claim for an item I bought at your shop?
- Where is your authorized service center for this brand?
- Can I return an item without the original receipt?
- How long does a warranty refund or replacement take?

### Delivery & E-commerce
- How long does delivery to Mwanza or Arusha take?
- What is the delivery charge for upcountry orders?
- Do you deliver to PO Box addresses or only physical locations?
- How can I track my order after placing it online?
- What happens if I'm not home when the delivery arrives?
- Can I change my delivery address after placing the order?
- Do you deliver on weekends and public holidays?

### Customs & Import
- Are the goods at your store imported through official channels?
- Has import duty been paid on these electronics?
- Can you provide a certificate of origin for these goods?
- What documentation comes with imported products to confirm they are genuine?

## Industry-Specific Escalation Triggers

1. Customer injured in-store due to wet floor, falling shelves, or broken equipment with no warning signage
2. Expired food product sold to customer causing illness or hospitalization
3. Counterfeit/substandard goods confirmed to present a safety hazard (electrical fire risk, toxic materials)
4. Physical altercation between security staff and customer on store premises
5. Evidence of systematic price fraud — consistently overcharging multiple customers at POS
6. Customer payment (M-Pesa or card) deducted but goods not provided and merchant unresponsive
7. Child labor or exploitative labor practices identified at a supplier or retail outlet
8. Large-scale data breach exposing customer loyalty card or payment information
9. TBS or TRA regulatory breach — confirmed sale of banned or duty-evading goods
10. Customer trapped or injured during store emergency due to blocked or unmarked fire exits
11. Confirmed theft of customer personal belongings from in-store lockers or changing rooms
12. Harassment or assault reported by female customer or staff member on store premises

## Disambiguation Notes

- **Retail vs. Food & Beverage**: If feedback mentions cooked food, restaurant meals, or beverage service, route to Food & Consumables. Retail covers packaged/sealed food products sold off the shelf.
- **Retail vs. Electronics**: Feedback specifically about smartphones, laptops, or technical devices should cross-reference the Electronics KB (20). Retail KB covers general merchandise stores that also stock electronics.
- **Retail vs. Logistics**: Complaints about delivery companies (DHL, G4S, courier firms) belong in Logistics KB (33). Retail KB covers delivery operated by the retail brand itself (e.g., store's own delivery rider).
- **Retail vs. Finance/Banking**: M-Pesa payment complaints linked to the merchant belong in Retail. M-Pesa account issues, float, or agent conduct belong in Finance/Banking KB (23).
- **Retail vs. Pharmacy**: Over-the-counter medicines sold at supermarket shelves are Retail context. Prescription dispensing, pharmacist advice, and drug-specific complaints belong in Pharmacy KB (19).
